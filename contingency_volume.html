Contingency Volume Calculation

This calculator computes as guidance the horizontal (Width) and vertical (Height) extent of the Contingency Volume based on the formulas in SORA 2.5 Annex A, section A.5.2.3. Input parameters below.

Input Parameters

Must be ≥ 3 m/s for multirotor.
Default: 1 s for manual intervention.
Selects calculation method upon entering CV.
Time from activation to full deployment.
Used for multirotor stop maneuver.
Used for fixed-wing turn maneuver.
Min height ≥ 3 * Characteristic Dimension.
Affects vertical error margin.
